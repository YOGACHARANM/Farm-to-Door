About Us

We connect local farmers with businesses and consumers, providing the freshest farm produce directly to your doorstep.

We are a platform dedicated to connecting local farmers directly with consumers and businesses. Our goal is to support sustainable farming practices while providing the freshest and healthiest produce available. By allowing farmers to sell directly to end-users, we eliminate middlemen, ensuring better prices for farmers and fresh produce for consumers.

We offer a unique combination of auction-based sales and subscription services, catering to individual needs and preferences. Join us in our mission to bring farm-fresh produce to everyone while supporting local farming communities.

Services

We offer weekly subscription plans, auction opportunities, and delivery services to bring farm-fresh produce to your door.

We offer a range of services designed to make fresh produce accessible and affordable:

  • Weekly Subscription Plans: Choose from various subscription plans tailored to suit families of all sizes. Enjoy handpicked fresh produce delivered to your doorstep every week.
  • Auction Platform: Bid on fresh produce and get the best deals directly from farmers. The auction platform is designed to be fair, transparent, and efficient, allowing you to access the freshest produce available.
  • Direct Farm Pickup: For those who want to experience farm life, we offer direct pickups from farms, allowing customers to choose their own fresh produce.
  • Delivery Services: Our logistics team ensures timely and safe delivery of all orders, guaranteeing that you receive fresh produce without any hassle.
